♡Projects♡

This page showcases my creative digital projects, inspired by soft aesthetics, gentle colors, and thoughtful design. Each project reflects my interest in combining creativity with computer-based tools.

Dresses / Casual Tops

I create soft and elegant clothing concepts such as dresses and tops, focusing on pastel colors, delicate details, and graceful silhouettes. These designs are made digitally and inspired by coquette and vintage aesthetics.

My goal is to express femininity and simplicity through fashion design, even in digital form.

Journal Covers

I design digital journal covers that feel calm, cozy, and personal. These covers are inspired by handwritten notes, ribbons, flowers, and soft textures. The color palettes are gentle, using pastels and light shades to create a soothing visual experience.

Each journal cover is created to make writing and planning feel more special and comforting.

Digital Stickers / Assets

I create digital stickers and decorative assets that can be used for digital planners, journals, or creative projects.

My goal is to express femininity and simplicity through fashion design, even in digital form.

Each journal cover is created to make writing and planning feel more special and comforting.

Packaging Design

I design packaging concepts for products such as cosmetics, stationery, or small gifts. The designs focus on soft colors, elegant typography, and delicate patterns to create an inviting and charming look.

These packaging designs aim to enhance the unboxing experience with a touch of gentle beauty.
